Our Mission

All Teens Financial Academy (ATFA) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide free, accessible financial literacy education to teenagers worldwide.

We believe that financial education is not just important—it's essential. Every teenager deserves to understand how money works, how to build wealth, and how to make smart financial decisions that will set them up for lifelong success.

Through our peer-to-peer learning approach, we make financial education engaging, relevant, and practical for today's generation.

What We Stand For

Our core values guide everything we do

🎯

Accessibility

Financial education should be free and available to every teenager, regardless of their background or circumstances.

👥

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Teens learn best from other teens. Our programs are designed and led by young people who understand the challenges and aspirations of their generation.

💡

Practical Application

We focus on real-world skills that students can immediately apply to their lives, from budgeting to investing to entrepreneurship.

🚀

Innovation

We continuously evolve our programs to meet the changing needs of modern teenagers and the financial landscape they'll inherit.

🌟

Empowerment

We believe in empowering young people to take control of their financial futures and become confident money managers.

🤝

Community

We build supportive communities where students can learn together, share experiences, and grow their financial knowledge.
