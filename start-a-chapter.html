How to Start Your Chapter

Follow these simple steps to bring ATFA's financial literacy program to your school and become a leader in the financial education movement.

1

Contact Us

Reach out to our team through our contact form or email. We'll schedule a call to discuss your school's needs and the chapter setup process.

2

Get School Approval

Work with your school administration to get approval for the ATFA chapter. We provide all necessary documentation and support materials.

3

Recruit Members

Build your chapter by recruiting passionate peers who want to learn and teach financial literacy. We help with promotional materials and strategies.

4

Launch Your Chapter

Begin hosting meetings, teaching courses, and empowering your peers with financial knowledge using our proven curriculum and resources.
